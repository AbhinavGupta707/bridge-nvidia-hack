# Demo Script

## Three-Minute Flow

1. Frame: "Bridge is a private, local advocate and record keeper for people
   navigating public services."
2. Start a housing/homelessness appointment with a limited-English resident.
3. Show two-way interpretation in the two-pane UI.
4. Trigger a cited homelessness policy card.
5. Trigger one resident question prompt.
6. Disable internet or switch to offline indicator.
7. Continue the appointment in local mode.
8. Generate the record and show citations, commitments, and next steps.

## Demo Safety Line

Bridge does not replace legal advice, clinical judgement, or a professional
interpreter for legally binding decisions. It surfaces cited public policy,
helps residents ask better questions, and gives them an evidence record.
